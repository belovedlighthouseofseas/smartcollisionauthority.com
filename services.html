Transparency

What Mobile Repair
Is Built For.

Mobile repair is the right fit for most cosmetic damage. Some situations need a full shop. We will tell you honestly which one applies.

Good Fit for Mobile
  • ✓Bumper cracks, dents, and scuffs
  • ✓Door and panel scratch repair
  • ✓Scuff and paint transfer removal
  • ✓Single-panel refinish and respray
  • ✓Paintless dent repair on eligible panels
  • ✓Keying and surface damage correction
  • ✓Cosmetic hail damage on hoods and roofs
May Need a Shop
  • –Frame or structural damage from collision
  • –Full multi-panel repaints requiring a booth
  • –Airbag or safety system repairs
  • –Damage that prevents the vehicle from running
  • –Insurance claims requiring shop documentation

If your situation falls in this list, we will let you know upfront rather than take the job and do it wrong.
